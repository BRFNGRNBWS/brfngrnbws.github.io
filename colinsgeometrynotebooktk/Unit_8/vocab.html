1. Dilation: A transformation that changes the size of a figure, but keeps the two figures similar.
  2. Ratio: A relationship between two numbers indicating how many times the first number contains the second.
  3. Proportion: The realationship between two things when the quatities of two are equal in ratios.
  4. Scale Factor: A number which scales, or multiplies, some quantity
