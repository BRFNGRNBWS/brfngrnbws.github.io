1. Regular: Being both equiangular and equilateral.
  2. Quadrilateral: A polygon with four edges.
  3. Parallelogram: A quadrilateral with two pairs of parallel sides.
  4. Trapezoid: A quadrilateral with at least one pair of parallel sides.
  5. Polygon: A plane figure with at least three straight sides and angles.
  6. Kite: A quadrilateral with adjacent pairs of sides congruent.
  7. Rectangle: A quadrilateral with four right angles and two pairs of congruent sides.
  8. Square: A quadrilateral with four congruent sides and four congruent angles.
  9. Pentagon: A five sided polygon.
  10. Hexagon: A six sided polygon.
  11. Septagon: A seven sided polygon (more commonly known as a heptagon).
  12. Octagon: An eight sided polygon.
  13. Nonagon: A nine sided polygon.
  14. Decagon: A ten sided polygon.
  15. Exterior Angle: An angle between a side of a figure and an adjacent side extended.
  16. Concave: An angle greater than 180°.
  17. Convex: An angle less than 180°
  18. Midsegment of Triangle: A line segment connecting the midpoints of two sides of a triangle.
  19. Midsegment of Trapezoid: A line segment connecting the midpoints of two non-parallel sides of a trapezoid.
