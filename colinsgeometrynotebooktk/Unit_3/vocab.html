1. Congruent: Coinciding exactly when superimposed
  2. Vertical Angles: Pairs of opposite angles made by two intersecting lines
  3. Alternate Interior Angles: The angles on opposite sides of a transversal
  4. Corresponding Angles: Angles that occupy the same relative position at each intersection where a straight line crosses two others
  5. Alternate Exterior Angles: Two exterior angles on opposite sides of a transversal
  6. Same Side Interior Angles: Two interior angles on the same side of a transversal
  7. Transversal: A line that intersects two or more lines
  8. Skew Lines: Two lines that don't intersect but that aren't parallel
  9. Supplementary Angles: Two angles whose sum is 180°
  10. Complimentary Angles: Two angles whose sum is 90°
