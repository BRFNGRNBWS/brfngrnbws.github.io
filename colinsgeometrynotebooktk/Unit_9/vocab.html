1. Arc: A closed segment of a differentiable curve.
  2. Arc Length: The distance along the arc.
  3. Central Angle: An angle that forms when to radii meet at the center of a circle.
  4. Chord: A straight line segment whose endpoints both lie on the circle.
  5. Circle: A closed shape that is the set of all points that are a given distance from a point.
  6. Concentric Circles: Circles with a common center.
  7. Degree Measure of Arc: The measure of the angle of an arc.
  8. Directrix: A line perpendicular to the axis of symmetry of a parabola.
  9. Focus: A point on the interior of a parabola, where the distance of it to a point on the parabola is equal to the distance from that point to the directrix.
  10. Great Circle: The largest 2-dimensional circle in a sphere.
  11. Inscribed Circles: The largest circle that can fit inside a plane figure.
  12. Major Arc: The arc of a circle with a greature measure than the minor arc.
  13. Minor Arc: The arc of a circle with a lesser measure than the major arc
  14. Point of Tangency: The single point at which a line touches a curved figure.
  15. Pythagorean Theorem: a2 + b2 = c2. Used to find the hypotenuse or either of the sides of a right triangle.
  16. Radian: A unit for measuring angles, equal to an angle at the center of a circle whose are is equal in length to the radius.
  17. Radius: Half of the diameter
  18. Secant: A line that intersects two points on a curve
  19. Sector: A part of a circle enclosed in two radii and the intercepter arc.
  20. Semicircle: A part of a circle consisting of half of the circle formed by the diameter.
  21. Similar: Having proportional sides and congruent angles.
  22. Tangent: A straight line that touches a curve at one point.
