1. Trigonomety: Branch of math dealing with triangles and their angles
  2. Sine Ratio: Opposite over hypotenuse
  3. Cosine Ratio: Adjacent over hypotenuse
  4. Tangent Ratio: Opposite over adjacent
  5. Adjacent Side: The two lines that make up an angle
  6. Opposite Side: A side directly across from either an angle or another side
  7. Hypotonuse: The longest side of a right triangle, opposite of the right angle
  8. Inverse Trigonomic Ratio: Determines angle when sides are know, instead of the other way around
  9. Law of Sines: Law used to find the sides and angles of a triangle using sine
  10. Law of Cosines: Law used to find the sides and angles of a traingle using cosine
  11. 30/60/90 Triangle: A triangle with the angles 30, 60, and 90 degrees
  12. 45/45/90 Triangle: A triangle with the angles 45, 45, and 90 degrees
  13. Similar Triangles: Triangles with all three angles congruent, regardless of side length
